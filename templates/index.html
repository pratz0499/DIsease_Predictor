About Disease Predictor

Disease Predictor is a medical report analysis prototype developed with the approach to improve the prediction of disease based on actual reports. This enables the users to get actual insight into their symptoms and possible diagnoses based on medical reports. Provides medical proffesionals to understand the possible tests to run and understand the case in depth. This can also be used as a helpful research tool to understand the taits of certain diseases based on the medical reports of specific zone. Disease Predictor uses an advanced predictive algorithm to process the data and predict the most probable disease that the user might encounter based on the symptoms entered.
